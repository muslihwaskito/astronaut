Astronaut Monkey

      Astronaut monkey is a space game that saves the mission of finding life outside the earth when the earth is destroyed, and the ecosystem in the solar system with the next planet, planet Gliese. Opal is the character of the monkey astronaut, players can earn tokens through skillful gameplay and contribution to the ecosystem. Players can explore the solar system, collect, raise, and build extraterrestrial life.
      Astronaut Monkey Mission was built a fun and educational way to introduce the world to blockchain technology. They immediately started working on Astronaut monkey to introduce the wonders of Blockchain technology to billions of players. Astronaut Monkey's Vision in the future there will be habitable planets for extraterrestrial life.
